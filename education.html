Education

  • 2009-2014

Primary School

In 2009, attendance began at SK Kampung Baharu Batu 16, Sungai Sumun. In 2014, he was transferred to SK Teluk Buloh, Hutan Melintang. In post-primary education, UPSR accomplishment comprised 2A, 1B, and 2C. In addition to academics, active tennis participation began in 2014, earning the honour of representing the Bagan Datuk area.

  • 2015-2019

Secondary School

Five years of education were completed in SMK Seri Perkasa, Hutan Melintang. The achievement contains a PT3 certificate with 4A, 1B, 1C, and 3D outcomes. In addition, an SPM certificate was earned with grades of 1A, 1A-, 2B+, 4B, and 1C+.

  • 2020-2022

Sijil Tinggi Pelajaran Malaysia

The pursuit of Sijil Tinggi Pelajaran Malaysia (STPM) was resumed at SMK Khir Johari, Sungai Sumun, beginning in 2020. Pengajian Am, Bahasa Melayu, Geography, and History were all part of the curriculum. Despite the hurdles created by the COVID-19 pandemic, all three semesters were completed in less than two years. This endeavour culminated in the achievement of the STPM Certificate, which was distinguished by a Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of 2.59 and a Malaysian University English Test (MUET) band score of 3.5.

  • 2023

Degree

At Puncak Perdana Campus, I am a second-semester Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) student specialising in Information Systems Management. Academic achievement in the first semester was highlighted by a Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.2.
